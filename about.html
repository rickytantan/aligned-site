We started with one question: why does the pain keep coming back?

Aligned was built for the patients who had tried everything temporary — massages that faded, painkillers that wore off, generic care that never asked why. Our answer is the Gonstead system: find the cause, correct it precisely, and teach you how to keep it corrected.

Movement is freedom.

Health is not the absence of symptoms — it is a body that moves the way it was designed to. That is the "new definition of health" in our name.

So we do not chase pain around your body. We examine the spine as a system, correct the segments that have stopped doing their job, and rebuild the strength that keeps them working. Care delivered with compassion, excellence and the professionalism you deserve.
