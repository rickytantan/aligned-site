Meet the team.

Dr Daniel Tan

Founder · Chiropractor · Gonstead System

Daniel believes recovery is a partnership. His consultations run long, on purpose — because a patient who understands their spine makes better decisions for it, long after they leave the clinic.

He built Aligned around patient education and the kind of careful, full-spine work that holds up over years, not weeks. When he is not adjusting spines, he is probably explaining one — with the X-ray on the screen and a model vertebra in hand.

  • Registered, Gonstead Chiropractic Society (Australia)
  • Member, Association of Chiropractic Malaysia
  • Focus: full-spine Gonstead, sports recovery, posture
  • Also leads our Animal Chiro care for dogs & cats
"If I can't explain why it hurts, I haven't finished examining."

Dr Sarah Lim

Chiropractor · Gonstead System · (sample profile)

Sarah is the one who gets the complicated histories. Patients with long, complicated histories tend to end up in her room — and leave with a shorter, clearer story about what is actually going on.

She is meticulous about the five criteria: if the evidence does not agree, she keeps examining until it does.

  • Doctor of Chiropractic
  • Focus: chronic pain, scoliosis, posture
"The spine always tells the truth — you just have to check all five ways."

Dr Marcus Wong

Chiropractor · Sports & Recovery · (sample profile)

A former competitive athlete, Marcus knows what it feels like to be told to "just rest". His work focuses on getting active bodies back to the sport they love — stronger than before the injury.

Expect him to watch how you move before he ever touches your spine.

  • Doctor of Chiropractic
  • Focus: sports injury, active recovery, rehab
"Rest treats the calendar. We treat the cause."

Jasmine Ooi

Rehab & Movement Therapist · (sample profile)

Jasmine takes over where the adjustment ends. Her rehab sessions rebuild the strength and movement habits that keep a corrected spine corrected — so results last beyond the clinic door.

Patients describe her sessions as tough, kind and strangely fun.

  • Certified rehabilitation therapist
  • Focus: strength, mobility, movement retraining
"The adjustment opens the door. Movement keeps it open."

Amanda Lee

Clinic Manager · Patient Care · (sample profile)

Amanda is the first voice you hear and the person who makes every visit run on time. From bookings to insurance paperwork to remembering how you take your appointment reminders, she has it handled.

If something about your visit could be smoother, she wants to know.

  • Your first point of contact, in English, 中文 & BM
  • Bookings, reports & insurance documents
"Great care should start before you reach the treatment room."
